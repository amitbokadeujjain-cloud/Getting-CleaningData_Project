Information about the data:
Data has 3 seperate datasets namely train and test data
These data contains the data of 6 activities performed by 30 volunteers.
The data is generated by the  accelerometer and gyroscope sensors in the Samsung Galaxy S II worn by the volunteers on their waist while performing the activities.
70% of the the data is available in the train dataset and 30% in the test dataset
Information about the data cleaning and processing as required for the project:
train and test dataset was prepared seperately from the 3 data files in each data set. column naming was done and both dataset were bind into a combined dataset after checking the preconditions for the bind operation.
columns containing mean and std text were extracted.
mean for each activity by each participant was calculated.
